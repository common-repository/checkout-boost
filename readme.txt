Customers share products on social media to get a free gift/discount/free-ship code. Add Countdown Timer, Exit Intent to offer.

== Description ==

This plugin is built to seamlessly connect your WooCommerce website to our <a href="https://beeketing.com/checkout-boost?utm_channel=applisting&utm_medium=woolisting&utm_fromapp=cboost” target="_blank">Checkout Boost</a> app on Beeketing platform. All features are configured and managed in our server, so the plugin is most lightweight as possible and takes minimum resources from your website.

Want to boost checkout rate and prevent cart abandonment? This plugin helps to offer your customers a free gift, a discount code, or a free-shipping coupon before they check out. If customers accept the offer, free gifts/discount codes/free-shipping codes will automatically be applied to their cart.

= HIGHLIGHTS: =

*  Countdown Timer: Make the offer urgent by adding a Countdown Clock. Customers are encouraged to complete purchase quickly within limited time to get the special free gift or coupon.
*  Exit-Intent Technology: Prevent abandoned carts by detecting customers who are about to leave the site, and display the offer.
*  Social Sharing: Ask customers to share products to Facebook/Twitter to get the offer as a reward - you can get more social referral traffic who see posts shared or tweeted by your current customers.
*  Post-purchase Upsell: Show offer after checkout is completed and Upsell more relevant items to motivate them to come back and buy more.

= FEATURES: =

5 Types of offers powered to help you deliver free gifts, discount & freeshipping coupons to customers in the most exciting ways:

####JUST-GET-IT:

Just give away the offer to your customers after they add the product to cart, no conditions.

####SHARE-TO-GET:

Offer displays asking customers to share the current product they are viewing to Facebook or Twitter. After customers share successfully, the offer of free gift/discount/free-ship code will be presented to them as a reward.

This way, you will get social referral traffic who see posts shared or tweeted by your current customers. The more shares you get, the more referral traffic you'll earn.

####COUNTDOWN-TIMER:

Want to create the sense of urgency and prompt customers to make spontaneous purchase? Limit a valid time to your offer, and add a live countdown clock to urge them to take action before time runs out!

####EXIT-INTET:

The exit-intent technology detects when your customers are about to leave the site by following their mouse's movement. With Exit-Intent offer, you can target leaving customers and convince them to remain and finish their order to receive a special offer.

####POST-PURCHASE UPSELL:

Offer a coupon code or free-shipping coupon for the next order at Thank-You page (after customers finish their purchase) to promote more promote purchases.

Based on what they've just bought, we can help you upsell more relevant items they might like to purchase as well.

= MORE CUSTOMIZATION SETTINGS: =

####Multiple Offers At the Same Time:

You can create many offers to run on different product pages at the same time. There is no limitation on how many offers you can run at the same time.

####Limit Offer Specific Products or Price Ranges:

Apply your offer to specific products or collections only, and/or within a specific price range. For example, you can create a 30% discount offer to New Arrivals collection only, and if customers buy more than $99.

Many conditions can be applied to 1 offer.

####Sales Motivator notification:

If your offer only applies to orders from mimimum cart value, customers who spend less than the price threshold will see a progress bar notifications that motivate them to spend more in order to reach the goal and receive the special offer.

####Minimized Icon at Site Corner:

When customers close offer popup, it will be minimized into a small icon at the site corner. Customers can click the icon to manually open the offer again anytime they want.

####Customize Offer Popup Themes:

We have designed 15+ different templates so you can choose the best ones that fit into your website's look & feel. You can use our pre-designed templates or customize your own.

####Holiday Themes Collection:

Holiday season is the best sales time of the year! We want your store to be colorful, merry, and beautiful on the holiday season, thus we pre-design many holiday themes to help you decorate your store, impress your customers and grow your sales.

The collection includes themes for Halloween, Thanksgiving, Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Christmas, New Year, Valentines,...

####Responsive on Desktop and Mobile:

Offer popups are designed to be beautifully responsive on any desktop screens and mobile devices (tablets and smart phones).

####Language Translation:

Default popups are in English. We support text translation to any languages.
